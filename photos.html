Photos

VeniceVIEW
The original line from Wrapped. The Venice Collection is our line of artist wrapping paper. These designs focus on the byproducts of creating fine art.

Animal StyleVIEW
This line of designer wrapping paper features exotic hides, skins, furs and phalanges. Our high quality cameras capture the natural essence and beautiful detail of the fur, giving it a three-dimensional feel. Wrap your gifts in exotic prints and take a walk on the wild side.

CarbonVIEW
Carbon is our high-end series based on the fundamental building blocks of life on earth.


Eye CandyVIEW
Eye candy is an experimental line where we reinvent classic stationery designs. We take conventional patterns, such as polka dots and stripes, and apply an artist’s touch to give the wrapping paper a custom, high-end feel. The result is a familiar design that is transformed into something truly extraordinary.

MetropolisVIEW
Metropolis is a series of luxury gift wrap that captures the abundance of beautiful patterns & textures that exist within the modern urban landscape.

Post CardsVIEW
Our newest line features high quality wrapping designs in the form of customizable postcard sets. Each postcard assortment showcases a hand-selected mix of Wrapped’s Venice, Animal Style, Carbon and Eye Candy lines. Each postcard features a clean writing canvas to cater your card to any occasion.


JournalsVIEW
Wrapped ventures in to designer journals. Each unique journal is a high resolution photograph. The inside pages take the design a step further with an original Wrapped image for each of the 8 styles. Really great journals from Wrapped.

Wall DecalsVIEW
Wall Decals from Wrapped + Blik as seen in The New York Times.

Gift TagsVIEW
Wrapped introduces gift tags. Each set comes complete with 6 unique designs, blank matte back for easy writing, and a 5" black and white string.


NotepadsVIEW
The Wrapped line of notepads offer an eye catching design to make your important notes stand out.

PlacementsVIEW
Wrapped brings you one time use tear-off paper placemat pads. Each pad comes with four colorful designs, in sequence, on a 48-sheet pad of uncoated paper. When you want to change the look of your table or your placemat gets dirty, simply tear off a clean sheet and repeat.
